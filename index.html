Roots and Shoots

Your next adventure is waiting!

Roots and Shoots is a fun, hands-on gardening community for curious kids, creative teens, and green-thumbed families. Whether you're planting your first seed or planning a backyard veggie patch, you'll find plenty of tips, seasonal ideas, and creative projects to help your garden grow. From sprouting seedlings on a windowsill to crafting with nature, every season brings new ways to explore and learn through gardening. Join in on seed swaps, garden days, and nature-themed events that everyone can enjoy.

Let’s grow something amazing together!
